Farming Tips & Tricks

🔥 Popular Tips

  • 🌱 Always prepare soil before planting
  • 💧 Water plants early morning
  • 🐄 Keep animals hydrated daily

🌱 Crop Farming Tips

Successful farming starts with good soil preparation and proper planting methods.

⭐ Important: Always test your soil before planting to know what nutrients are needed.

Use compost or manure to improve fertility. Ensure correct spacing between crops to avoid overcrowding. Water plants early morning or late afternoon to reduce evaporation.

Use natural pest control methods like crop rotation and organic sprays. Harvest crops at the right time and store them properly to maintain quality.

🐄 Animal Farming Tips

Animal farming requires proper feeding, housing, and health management.

⭐ Important: Clean water must always be available for animals.

Provide balanced nutrition including proteins and vitamins. Keep animal shelters clean to prevent diseases and infections.

Vaccinate animals regularly and monitor their health daily. Select strong animals for breeding to improve productivity.
